This haiku encapsulates the essence of collective purpose and the power of love. The opening line, 'That is a great thought,' sets a reflective tone, suggesting that the speaker is acknowledging a profound realization. The second line, 'You are working for the whole,' emphasizes the importance of community and interconnectedness, highlighting that individual efforts contribute to a larger good. Finally, 'Love everything' serves as a powerful call to action, urging an all-encompassing appreciation and compassion for life and the world around us. Together, these lines convey a message of positivity, urging readers to recognize their impact on the collective and to embrace love as a guiding principle in all endeavors.
