This haiku captures a poignant moment of entrusting one's children to the care of another, invoking a sense of trust and continuity. The imagery of 'wind and sunshine' suggests the natural elements that will surround and nurture the children, symbolizing both the challenges and joys they will face. The poem reflects a deep connection between human relationships and the natural world, emphasizing the cyclical nature of life and the enduring presence of nature as a guardian.
