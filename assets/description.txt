This haiku captures a moment of introspection and realization. The speaker reflects on their intentions and actions, recognizing the sincerity of their past efforts. The phrase 'I looked about me' suggests a pause to take stock of their surroundings and inner state. The realization that they 'had meant to be' true indicates a commitment to authenticity. The final line, 'All so clear and plain,' signifies a moment of enlightenment where everything becomes transparent and straightforward. This haiku beautifully encapsulates the simplicity and profundity of self-awareness and truth.
