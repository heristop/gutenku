This haiku paints a vivid picture of a moment where the past and present converge through the medium of music. The phrase 'Who came up to him' suggests an encounter, possibly with a person or a memory. 'With broad waves of melody' evokes the powerful and encompassing nature of music, which can transport us to different times and places. Finally, 'Living the old life' implies that through this melody, the individual is momentarily reliving experiences from the past. The haiku captures the timeless and evocative power of music to bridge different eras of our lives.
