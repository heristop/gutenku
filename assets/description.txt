This haiku captures a moment of surprise and realization. The poem begins with an image of something seemingly lost in thought, which creates a sense of calm and introspection. The second line introduces a sudden movement, as the subject buzzes over the speaker's head, suggesting a shift from tranquility to awareness. The final line reveals a reaction, as 'she flushed,' indicating a moment of embarrassment or surprise. This haiku beautifully encapsulates the fleeting nature of unexpected encounters and the emotions they evoke.
