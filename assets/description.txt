This haiku captures a moment of transition and opportunity. The first line, 'The tide had just turned,' suggests a change in circumstances, perhaps symbolizing a new beginning or a shift in fortune. The second line, 'The wood was pretty open,' evokes a sense of clarity and possibility, as if the path ahead is clear and inviting. The final line, 'With this to help me,' implies that the speaker feels supported or encouraged by these changes. Overall, the haiku reflects a sense of optimism and readiness to embrace new opportunities as they arise.
