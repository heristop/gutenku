This haiku captures a moment of serene beauty and contemplation following a rainstorm. The first line, 'The rain was over,' sets the scene by indicating the end of a natural event, suggesting a sense of renewal and calm. The second line, 'A very handsome filly,' introduces a striking image of a young horse, symbolizing vitality and grace. The final line, 'Everard wondered,' adds a layer of introspection, as the character Everard reflects on the scene before him. This haiku beautifully intertwines nature, beauty, and human thought, inviting readers to pause and ponder the simple yet profound moments in life.
