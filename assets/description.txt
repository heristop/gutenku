This haiku captures a moment of unity and exhilaration during a hunt. The first line, 'Other hounds joined in,' sets the scene with the image of dogs coming together, suggesting a sense of community and shared purpose. The second line, 'The masters rode together,' emphasizes the camaraderie among the hunters, riding in unison. The final line, 'Ecstatically,' conveys the intense joy and excitement felt by both the hounds and their masters. The haiku encapsulates a fleeting yet vivid moment of collective enthusiasm and harmony in nature.
