This haiku captures a moment of reflection and anticipation, suggesting a shared experience of being placed in a rowboat, which symbolizes a journey through life. The phrase 'Let me know in time' implies a desire for communication and connection, as well as a hint of uncertainty about the future. The imagery of 'wind and sunshine' evokes a sense of freedom and natural beauty, contrasting with the potential challenges of navigating the waters ahead. Overall, the haiku conveys themes of companionship, the passage of time, and the delicate balance between hope and uncertainty.
