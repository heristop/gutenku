This haiku encapsulates the beauty and rejuvenating qualities of a morning by the sea. The first line, 'They praised the morning,' suggests a communal appreciation of the dawn, setting a tone of gratitude and reverence for nature. The second line, 'The sea-air always does good,' emphasizes the restorative power of the ocean breeze, hinting at its ability to refresh both body and spirit. Lastly, 'A glance of brightness' evokes a visual image of sunlight glimmering on the water, symbolizing hope and positivity. Together, these lines create a harmonious image of tranquility, inviting readers to reflect on the simple yet profound joys found in nature.
