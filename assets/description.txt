This haiku captures a moment of universal appreciation and joy. The speaker observes a collective enjoyment, suggesting that 'they'—perhaps people, animals, or even nature itself—find something immensely pleasant. The phrase 'the whole big dear world' evokes a sense of unity and affection for the world, highlighting a shared experience of happiness. The simplicity of the haiku mirrors the simplicity of the joy it describes, emphasizing how small moments can resonate on a global scale.
