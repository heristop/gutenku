📗 The divine comedy by Dante Alighieri

~~~

darting forth a prong
turning to my guide his face
vessel of all guile

~~~

se tournant vers mon guide, son visage
vase de toute ruse

michi-biki no kao o furikaeri
subete no azamuki no utsuwa
