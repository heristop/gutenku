📗 The Fortunate Calamity by Pansy

~~~

every one but me
if they knew it long before
The plans were all made

~~~

🇫🇷 tous sauf moi
s’ils le savaient depuis longtemps
les plans étaient faits

🇯🇵 minna boku igai
ano toki kara shitte ita nara
keikaku wa subete kimatte ita

~~~
