📗 Twenty years after by Alexandre Dumas

~~~

turned slowly around
on seeing the young man
Cromwell raised his head

~~~

🇫🇷 se retournant lentement
à la vue du jeune homme
Cromwell leva la tête

🇯🇵 shizuka ni furimuite
wakai otoko o miru to
kuromuweru wa atama o ageta
